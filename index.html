How We Work

A process built for clarity, not confusion.

Four structured phases. One goal: your project modeled right the first time.

01

Brief & Scope

We start with a call. You share drawings, specs, and goals. We map the scope, LOD requirements, and timeline - everything documented before we start.

02

BIM Modeling

We build the model discipline-by-discipline - Architectural first, then Structural and MEP - maintaining consistent naming standards and shared coordinates throughout.

03

Coordination & Clash Detection

All disciplines are federated. We run automated clash detection, resolve conflicts, and issue a clear coordination report - nothing left to chance on site.

04

Delivery & Support

Final model, drawings, and reports delivered in your preferred format. We stay available for revisions, RFIs, and design changes throughout the project lifecycle.
